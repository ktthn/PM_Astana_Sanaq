ASTANA SANAQ 2023

Astana Sanaq 2023 is a dedicated organization committed to conducting the population census in Kazakhstan's capital city, Astana. Our team of experts specializes in data collection and analysis, aiming to provide accurate and comprehensive demographic information to support informed decision-making for the government and society.

OUR MISSION

To conduct the most comprehensive and accurate population census in Kazakhstan, providing essential demographic data for the country's development and policy planning.
